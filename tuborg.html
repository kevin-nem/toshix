About Tuborg

Tuborg is one of Denmark's most iconic beer brands, representing over 150 years of brewing excellence and Danish craftsmanship. Founded in 1873 in Hellerup, just north of Copenhagen, Tuborg has grown from a local Danish brewery to an internationally recognized premium beer brand, beloved by beer enthusiasts around the world.

What sets Tuborg apart is not just its rich heritage, but its unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and the Danish tradition of brewing exceptional beer. Every bottle of Tuborg represents the perfect balance of traditional brewing methods and modern techniques, creating a beer that is both authentic and refreshingly contemporary.

Our Rich History

The Beginning (1873)

Tuborg was established by Philip W. Heyman, who had a vision to create Denmark's finest beer. The brewery was strategically located in Hellerup, taking advantage of the area's excellent water sources and proximity to Copenhagen's growing market.

Innovation & Growth

Throughout its history, Tuborg has been at the forefront of brewing innovation. The company was among the first to adopt new brewing technologies and quality control methods, ensuring that every beer met the highest standards of excellence.

International Expansion

From its Danish roots, Tuborg expanded internationally, bringing authentic Danish brewing tradition to beer lovers worldwide. Today, Tuborg is enjoyed in over 70 countries, making it one of Denmark's most successful export brands.

What Makes Tuborg Beer So Good

Perfect Balance

Tuborg achieves the perfect harmony between hoppy bitterness and malty sweetness, creating a refreshing and satisfying taste that appeals to both casual drinkers and beer connoisseurs.

Crisp & Clean Finish

Every sip of Tuborg delivers a crisp, clean finish that leaves you refreshed and wanting more. This signature characteristic is achieved through meticulous brewing and filtration processes.

Premium Water Source

Tuborg uses Denmark's pristine water sources, which are naturally soft and pure, contributing significantly to the beer's exceptional taste and quality.

Our Premium Beer Selection

Tuborg Green

The flagship beer that started it all. Tuborg Green is a premium pilsner with a crisp, refreshing taste and a perfect balance of hops and malt. Its distinctive green bottle and label have become iconic symbols of quality and Danish brewing excellence.

Tuborg Gold

A premium lager with a rich, golden color and a smooth, full-bodied taste. Tuborg Gold represents the pinnacle of Danish brewing craftsmanship, offering a more robust flavor profile while maintaining the brand's signature quality.

Tuborg Red

A unique beer with a distinctive character, Tuborg Red offers a slightly stronger taste with more pronounced hop notes, perfect for those who appreciate a beer with more complexity and depth.
